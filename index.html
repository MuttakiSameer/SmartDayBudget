Pay Yourself First Calculator

Reverse budgeting flips traditional finance by prioritizing savings before expenses. A pay yourself first calculator automates this philosophy, instantly securing your wealth-building goals the moment you get paid. It calculates exactly what remains for daily spending only after your future is funded.

Sinking Fund Planning

Mastering your daily allowance organically accelerates effective sinking fund planning. By strictly managing your available everyday cash flow, you naturally prevent overspending. This discipline ensures those dedicated monthly contributions for future irregular expenses, like vacations or repairs, remain completely protected and fully funded.
