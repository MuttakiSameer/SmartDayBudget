What Is a Daily Budget Calculator?

A daily budget calculator is the fastest way to know exactly how much you can spend today. It takes your income, subtracts your savings and bills, then divides what remains by the days in your month. No spreadsheet. No guessing. Just your number.

Unlike traditional budgeting apps, this reverse budgeting calculator does not require you to link your bank account. Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is ever stored on a server.

How Reverse Budgeting Works

Traditional budgeting says: earn, spend, save whatever is left. Reverse budgeting flips this. You set your savings target first — 10%, 20%, or a fixed amount. That money is "paid" to yourself immediately. The rest is your guilt-free spending pool.

This pay yourself first calculator automates that logic in seconds. Enter your income. Set your savings target. See your daily spending allowance. Done.

Who Should Use This Calculator?

This daily budget calculator is perfect for freelancers, students, gig workers, and anyone with variable income. If your paycheck is not the same every month, traditional budgets break down. A daily allowance keeps you on track regardless of income swings.

It also works well for people trying to hit a specific savings goal or those tackling credit card debt — because knowing your daily limit prevents overspending that derails your plan.

How To Calculate Your Daily Allowance

Formula: Daily Allowance = (Income − Savings − Fixed Expenses) ÷ Days

For example: Income $4,000 / month. Savings target 20% = $800. Rent + bills = $1,400. Remaining = $1,800 ÷ 30 days = $60 per day to spend freely.

Our daily spending allowance calculator does this math automatically, live, as you type.
